Internationalisation

UI labels (Apply, Cancel, Days/Months/Years, Start/End time) are provided via the DUAL_CALENDAR_INTL injection token with English defaults. Override it at any injector level. Calendar day/month names are delegated to Angular Material's MAT_DATE_LOCALE.

French labels — component-level provider

Open the picker and look at the Apply/Cancel buttons and mode selector labels — they are rendered in French via a local DUAL_CALENDAR_INTL override.

Calendar locale — MAT_DATE_LOCALE

Day and month names inside mat-calendar are controlled by Angular Material's own date adapter locale. Provide MAT_DATE_LOCALE at the app or component level.
